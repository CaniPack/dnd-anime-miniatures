Frequently Asked Questions

Are these STL files good for Dungeons & Dragons miniatures?

Yes — every model is sized to print at 32mm scale (1.7% in your slicer), which is the standard for D&D 5e and Pathfinder tabletop grids. Drop them in as PCs, NPCs, or unique encounter villains. Each product page suggests a D&D class fit based on the character's abilities.

What do I get when I buy a One Piece STL file?

You get a digital .STL file (or a Google Drive link for bundles and large files) that you can 3D print at home. Files are instant-download after purchase via your Etsy account. No physical product is shipped.

Can I print these on FDM or resin printers?

Yes — every model is compatible with both FDM (PLA/PETG) and resin (LCD/SLA/DLP). Recommended settings: Resin 0.05mm layer with 1.5-3s exposure; FDM 0.1-0.2mm layer with 5–15% infill. Each listing includes detailed print recommendations.

What scale can I print these at?

Every model comes at real human scale (~1.9m / 6.2ft) so you can scale freely in your slicer. Quick reference: 32mm tabletop / DND mini = 1.7% scale, 75mm collectible = 3.9%, 150mm bust = 7.9%, 200mm display = 10.5%.

Are supports included in the STL file?

No — models come without supports so you can generate supports tuned to your printer and slicer settings. Recommended orientation: vertical with supports from the base.

Can I sell prints I make from these files?

Yes. The license allows personal use AND physical sale of printed figures. You can print and sell the physical figure at conventions, online, or anywhere. Redistribution or resale of the digital STL files is NOT permitted.

How big are the files?

Individual STLs range from ~5–45 MB. Larger files (above Etsy's 20MB upload limit) are delivered as a small ZIP containing a Google Drive download link. Bundles contain 3–17 character models and are delivered via Drive.

Do you take requests for new One Piece characters?

Yes — message us on Etsy with the character you'd like to see. We prioritize characters from arcs where we already have models, and we ship new listings regularly.
