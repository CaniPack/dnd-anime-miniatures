Questions about Roronoa Zoro

What scale should I print Roronoa Zoro at?

The model is at real human scale (~1.9m). Common print scales: 32mm tabletop = 1.7%, 75mm collectible = 3.9%, 150mm bust = 7.9%, 200mm display = 10.5%. Set the scale in your slicer before printing.

What's the mesh quality of Roronoa Zoro?

The model has 383k triangles — high enough detail for resin printing at small scales, and clean enough for FDM at medium-large scales.

Does Roronoa Zoro print well on FDM printers?

Yes. Recommended FDM settings: 0.1-0.2mm layer height, 5% infill for small scaled prints or 15% for larger ones. Print vertically with supports from the base.

Is Roronoa Zoro supported for resin printing?

Yes. The mesh is optimized for resin at small to medium scales. Recommended settings: 0.05mm layer, 1.5-3s exposure, light supports from the base. The model is solid (not hollowed) — hollow it in your slicer to save resin.

Can I sell physical prints of Roronoa Zoro?

Yes — the license allows you to print and sell the physical figure (conventions, online, custom commissions). What you can NOT do is redistribute or resell the digital STL file itself.
